    Why Waste Not:

  1. 1.3 billion tons of food are wasted every year with and this amounts to US$1 trillion dollars of wasted or lost food.
  2. If wasted food was a country, it would be the third largest producer of carbon dioxide in the world, after the United States and China.
  3. Just one quarter of all wasted food could feed the 795 million undernourished people around the world who suffer from hunger.
  4. In the USA, 30-40% of the food supply is wasted, equaling more than 20 pounds of food per person per month.

Waste Not was created to bring attention to food waste in America. We developed this application to encourage individuals and families to create meals with items they currently have in their kitchen. By wasting less food not only do you save money but also reduces your carbon footprint in landfills.
